Overview

Purpose

The purpose for the website is to showcase works of art pieces. This will be done in an elegant way using effective colors and fonts. Users can click on each image and be taken to a separate page with more information.

Audience

All that are interested in art pieces, but primarily adults 30+.

Content

Home page

The landing page will be made so that if they have no clue what it’s all about, users will be informed visually and literally about the short and sweet. In this websites case, it’ll be a showcase of highlighted artworks, and simple introduction paired with some buzz words. As they become familiar, they can navigate to what sparks their interest the most, the artist, the artworks, or a specific art piece that caught their eye.

Images for the Home page

The artist

This page will provide a picture of the artist which is AI generated. Some descriptions about their history as an artist, their interests, and what inspires them for their creations.

Images for the Page 2

Gallery

The gallery page will provide images, art name, and descriptions(briefly) of all art pieces. It’ll be organized to be scrolled down for more pieces. Each image should be a clickable link to take them to the individual art page.
